# 1.CyberSecurity Standards & FramesWorks

- These are the frameworks provides us the guideline or rules for best practices.

- What to protect and how to protect
- How to respond to attacks
- Managing the risk

## Types of frameworks
    1. ISO: International Organization for Standardization. 
    Globally for ISMS.
   Example: Encrypted records in the Google pay server.

    2. NIST Frameworks:
        0.Identify
            1.Protect
            2.Detect
            3.Respond
            4.Recover
    Example: Money spliting,Fraud Money Deposit
    3. CIS:
    It stands for Centre for Internet Security.

    - Inventory of devices
    - Secure Configurations
    - Malware Defence
    - Filtering Security

    4. GDPR 
        - Protection of data of Individuals.
        - Company should define the range of accessibilty.
        - Misuse results in fines
    Example: Whatsapp,FB
